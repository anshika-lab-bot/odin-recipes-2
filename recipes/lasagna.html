World's Best Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is packed with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and cheesy goodness!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion, garlic, olive oil
  • Ricotta cheese, mozzarella, parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Cook the meat with sauce and seasonings.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake at 375°F for 45 minutes.
  5. Let it cool and enjoy!
