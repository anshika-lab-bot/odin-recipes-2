Decadent Chocolate Soufflé

Description

This rich and fluffy chocolate soufflé is the perfect dessert to impress guests or treat yourself. Light on the outside, gooey in the center!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter (plus extra for ramekins)
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 3 egg whites
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Butter and sugar the inside of 4 ramekins.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan, stir in flour to form a roux, then gradually whisk in milk until smooth and thickened.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in chopped chocolate until melted. Mix in egg yolks.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites with salt until soft peaks form. Gradually add sugar and beat until stiff peaks form.
  5. Gently fold the whites into the chocolate mixture.
  6. Spoon into ramekins and bake for 12–15 minutes until risen but still slightly soft in the center.
  7. Dust with powdered sugar and serve immediately!
